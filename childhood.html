My Childhood Journey

I was born and raised in Kauriya, a small and beautiful village surrounded by nature, peace, and simplicity. As a child, I was always curious and full of imagination — I loved exploring new things, opening toys, and wondering how they actually worked. I still remember the day I first saw a computer; it felt like magic, and that moment sparked my lifelong interest in technology and creativity. My childhood in Kauriya taught me the importance of curiosity, patience, and the joy of learning — values that continue to guide me in my journey as an engineering student today.
